Most small business websites look like a business card. The ones that grow revenue work like a sales team.

The difference between those two outcomes is not budget — it is strategy. A website built with the right foundation does not just represent your business. It attracts customers, establishes credibility, and generates revenue around the clock — even when you are not working.

CMET Nation is launching with a workshop designed to show you exactly how to build that website.

## What You Will Learn

This workshop is built for entrepreneurs who are ready to stop treating their website as a cost and start treating it as an investment. Every topic connects directly to one outcome — growing your business.

- How to structure your website so it converts visitors into paying customers
- What every revenue-generating small business website must include — and what most get dangerously wrong
- How to build trust and credibility online so potential customers choose you before they ever contact you
- The biggest website mistakes entrepreneurs make that are costing them customers and revenue — and exactly how to fix them
- How to design for mobile users — because that is where your customers are finding you
- Search engine optimization (SEO) fundamentals so your ideal customers can find your business when they are ready to buy
- How AI and today's technology tools can help you build and maintain a professional, revenue-ready website without a large budget
- Website security basics to protect your business, your customers, and your reputation

## Ask Me Anything

Following the workshop, I will host a live AMA — Ask Me Anything — session. Bring your questions. Bring your challenges. Bring your website. This is your opportunity to get direct, expert guidance on your specific business situation from a practitioner who has built what he teaches.

## About CMET

The Center for Micro-Entrepreneurial Training (CMET) is a nonprofit training institute headquartered in Orlando, Florida, with a clear and urgent mission — to prepare, connect, and support Black, Brown, and women entrepreneurs with the knowledge, resources, and networks to start, grow, or acquire sustainable businesses. CMET provides high-impact, skills-based training rooted in five essential pillars — Business Planning, Financial Literacy, Target Audience Definition, Branding, and Marketing.
